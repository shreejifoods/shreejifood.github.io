Meet Our Chef

Riddhi
Owner & Chef
Welcome to Shreeji Food & Snacks (also known as Sriji Food, Shriji Food, Sreeji Food)! I'm Riddhi Khunt, and food has always been my passion. From crafting authentic Gujarati, Punjabi, and Indo-Chinese dishes to exploring new culinary creations, I take pride in delivering quality, tasty, and hygienic meals.

As a lifelong vegetarian and vegan food enthusiast, I focus on using the freshest ingredients, ensuring every dish is both wholesome and delicious.

I love experimenting with new recipes and believe that cooking is an evolving art. Inspired by my favorite culinary shows like MasterChef, I am constantly learning and refining my skills. Your feedback is invaluable to me, and I'm always ready for new challenges, whether it's catering a big event or creating something special for your party.

At Shreeji Food & Snacks (Sriji Food, Shriji Food, Sreeji Food), we are dedicated to making every meal an experience to savor.
